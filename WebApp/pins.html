Blizzard collectible Pins

Blizzard pins are one of the hottest collectables avalabile at Blizzcon. There are several different types of pins: series, cute but deadly, Esports, DarkMoon faire exclusives, and many more. Check out bellow to find information about each pin.

Blizzard series sets

Orignally released at Blizzcon in 2014 the set contains 10 unique colored pins along with a gold version of each pin.
(mouse over image to see gold versions)

  • Arthas
  • Diablo
  • Illidan
  • Kerigan
  • Malfurion
  • Murky
  • Nova
  • Sylvanas
  • Tyrael
  • Zeratul
