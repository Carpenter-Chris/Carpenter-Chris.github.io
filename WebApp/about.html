About us:

Stay awhile and listen...

We are a group of die hard fans that love Blizzard (even if they are making it hard to love them these last few years). I have lucky enough to have attended every Bizzcon since 2009. The first several years I was a Casually collector of blizzcon merch. Started getting into pin collecting in 2016. Now Blizzcon feels like Darkmoon faire-trade-con. A few of my close friends are consulting on this site, they are also avid Blizzcon attendies.
